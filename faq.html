FAQ

How does sizing run?

Heavyweight and oversized. Size down one for a closer fit. See the size guide.

When will my order ship?

In-stock pieces ship in 1–3 business days. Made-to-order pieces are produced first and typically ship in 5–10 business days. You'll get tracking by email.

What's your return policy?

Unworn items with tags can be returned within 30 days. See Returns.

Is checkout secure?

Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Where do you ship?

Across the US (and Canada). More regions coming.

How do I reach you?

Email hello@zoemain.fit.

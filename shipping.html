Shipping & Returns

Last updated: June 2026

Order processing

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when your order is placed and a tracking link once it ships. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

Shipping methods & delivery

We ship across the United States via tracked carriers. Estimated delivery is typically 3–7 business days after dispatch, depending on your location. Delivery estimates are not guaranteed and may be affected by the carrier or factors outside our control.

Shipping costs

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your order and destination. Any promotions affecting shipping will be shown at checkout.

International orders

If international shipping is available at checkout for your country, please note that any customs duties, taxes, or import fees are the responsibility of the recipient.

Lost or delayed packages

If your tracking hasn't updated or your order hasn't arrived within the estimated window, email us at hello@zoemain.fit and we'll help track it down.

Returns

Return window

We want you to keep your ZOEMAIN piece for years. If something isn't right, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery.

Condition

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with any tags attached. For hygiene reasons, jewelry and final-sale items are not eligible for return unless they arrive damaged or faulty.

How to start a return

Email hello@zoemain.fit with your order number and the item you'd like to return, and we'll send you instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method through Stripe. Refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the item was faulty or incorrect.

Exchanges

Need a different size? Start a return for the original item and place a new order, or email us and we'll help arrange an exchange while stock lasts.

Faulty or incorrect items

If you receive an item that is damaged, defective, or not what you ordered, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right at no cost to you.

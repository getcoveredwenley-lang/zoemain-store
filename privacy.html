Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ZOEMAIN ("ZOEMAIN," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect when you visit zoemain.fit or place an order, how we use and share it, and the rights you have. By using our site, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us and information collected automatically:

  • Order & account information — your name, email address, shipping and billing address, phone number, and order details.
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider, Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Email sign-ups — the email address you provide to receive drops and updates.
  • Device & usage data — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and similar analytics data collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use your information

  • To process, fulfill, and ship your orders, and to send order confirmations and updates.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To send marketing and new-release emails when you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To prevent fraud, secure our site, and meet our legal and tax obligations.
  • To understand and improve how our store performs.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us run the business, and only as needed:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Shipping & fulfillment partners — to deliver your order.
  • Email & analytics providers — to send communications you opt into and measure site performance.

We may also disclose information when required by law, or to protect our rights, customers, or the public.

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep your cart working, remember preferences, and understand site traffic. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as well.

Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, and to opt out of marketing or the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Residents of California and other U.S. states with privacy laws, and visitors in the EEA/UK under the GDPR, have these rights. To exercise any of them, email us at hello@zoemain.fit and we will respond within the time required by law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, after which we securely delete or anonymize it.

Children's privacy

Our store is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello@zoemain.fit.
